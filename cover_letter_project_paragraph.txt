In my recent analytics project, I conducted a complete end‑to‑end analysis of epilepsy seizure patterns using Python, Pandas, Seaborn, and Matplotlib. I cleaned and structured the dataset, explored trends over time, examined correlations between sleep deviation and seizure frequency, and developed clear, insightful visualizations to communicate findings. This project strengthened my ability to work with real‑world data, build reproducible analysis pipelines, and translate complex patterns into actionable insights—skills I am excited to bring into a data‑driven role.
