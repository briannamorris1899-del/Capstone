This project analyzes epilepsy seizure patterns using Python to uncover trends, correlations, and potential contributing factors. I cleaned and structured the dataset, explored seizure frequency over time, examined relationships between sleep deviation, adherence, and seizure count, and created clear visualizations to communicate insights. The project demonstrates my ability to work with real-world data, build reproducible analysis workflows, and translate findings into meaningful conclusions. It reflects my growing expertise in Python, Pandas, Seaborn, and Matplotlib, as well as my commitment to developing strong analytical and problem‑solving skills.
